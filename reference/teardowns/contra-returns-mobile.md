# Teardown — Contra Returns (mobile, 2021+) · Konami/TiMi/Garena

**Role in corpus:** the **official mobile Contra** — the goal explicitly names "Contra
Returns/Evolution" as a recent web/Android Contra-like. It is the touch-era product a
mobile/browser player most directly compares OUR game against. Previously the corpus's
biggest title gap (console/pixel tier was covered; the official *mobile* Contra was not).
Frames: `frames/contra-returns-mobile/` (official store gameplay screenshots).
Written by **looking** at real gameplay screenshots.

---

## What it actually is (observed)
Contra Returns is a **3D-HD reimagining with a heavy free-to-play touch UI** — NOT pixel art.
- **Art tier:** fully **3D-rendered** — a detailed 3D **Bill Rizer** (blonde, bandana, ammo
  belt) on 3D jungle platforms; lush 3D backgrounds (waterfalls, palms, mountains, depth-of-
  field). High production, but a *modern-3D-mobile* look, not retro pixel.
- **HUD / UI:** **cluttered F2P overlay** — virtual d-pad, JUMP button, grenade + special-move
  icons, per-weapon ammo counters, "Low Ammo" warnings, boss HP bars w/ shields, **operator-
  dialogue portraits** ("Operator Tina — reinforcements have arrived"), mission objectives
  ("Complete the Stage / Clear within 5 min / Score 100"). Gacha/live-service framing.
- **Content:** the classic Contra **jungle Stage 1** reimagined; reinforcement helicopters,
  turret-mechs, 200+ levels, PvP modes, unlockable heroes/weapons/skins, legion/pet systems.

## Per-dimension read (vs OUR game)
| dim | Contra Returns | OUR game |
|-----|----------------|----------|
| Sprite/anim | 3D-HD models (high fidelity, non-pixel) | 16-bit pixel (retro-authentic) |
| Hit-feedback/juice | big 3D explosions, screen FX, F2P popups | pixel muzzle/spark/hit-stop, telegraphs |
| Movement | run/jump/prone via touch stick | keyboard, fixed-arc arcade jump |
| Weapon juice | 20+ weapons, HD VFX | M/S/L/F arcade lineage, distinct pixel signatures |
| Enemy density | busy 3D scenes + reinforcements | 3–5 concurrent + aerial/turret variety |

## POSITIONING INSIGHT (the important one)
The **official mobile Contra went 3D-HD + gacha/F2P UI**, not pixel. This reframes "prefer
over today's popular web/Android Contra-likes":
- OUR game does **not** compete with Contra Returns on *3D-HD spectacle or content volume* —
  it can't and shouldn't. It competes on a **different, deliberate value proposition**:
  **nostalgic pixel-arcade feel + a clean, uncluttered arcade HUD + zero-friction instant
  browser play (no download, no F2P store, no gacha).**
- **OUR clean arcade HUD is an ADVANTAGE** here: Contra Returns' screen is dense with touch
  controls, ammo meters, dialogue portraits, and monetization overlays; OUR SCORE/LIVES/weapon
  HUD over pure gameplay reads far more *"1987 arcade"* and less cluttered.
- **The pixel-art revival tier (Blazing Chrome / Gunslugs) is OUR real head-to-head**, not
  Returns — a player who wants *nostalgic pixel Contra in a browser* is choosing between OURS
  and those, and would bounce off Returns' 3D-gacha-mobile framing. OUR one-hit "1987 way"
  ARCADE mode + spread-fan + fixed-arc jump target exactly that player.

**Consequence for the fidelity bar:** keep the confirmed target = the pixel/indie tier
(Gunslugs-class); do NOT chase Contra Returns' 3D-HD look (off-strategy). The corpus's
"matches/exceeds" verdict should be read as *within the retro-pixel run-and-gun tier*, where
the clean-HUD + instant-play + arcade-purity of OUR game is a real differentiator vs the
cluttered official mobile product.
